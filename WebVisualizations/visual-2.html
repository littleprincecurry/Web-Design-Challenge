    Anime Sources


    The source field on MyAnimeList refers to what the anime is based on. Looking at my graph, you can see the majority of titles are either based on a manga or are an original property. From my own knowledge I can say this would be true for anime as a whole, but in the future I'd like to compare my personal data to the entirety of the dataset.

    You can also see there are a number of sources marked as 'Unknown' in the dataset. I would assume this to be titles where the source field has not been filled in, it's not exactly a mystery to determine what a title's source might be. I would have to look further into which titles this encompasses before deciding if the data can be adjusted or if it's worth leaving out.
