    Anime per Type


    Type here refers to where a title original aired. Some are self explanatory, such as TV and movie, but others may be unfamiliar with those without prior knowledge. OVA (Original Video Animation) refers to titles that were released straight to home video (VHS, DVD, even LaserDisc,) while ONA (Orignal Net Animation) refers to titles that premiered originally on the internet. This includes titles that premier on streaming services such as Netflix, and thus I predict that slice will grow bigger over time, while the OVA slice shrinks.

    The results here aren't surprising, although in the future I'd like to compare this against the total database. I suspsect TV would take an even bigger slice of the pie, as it were, but we live in uncertain times!
