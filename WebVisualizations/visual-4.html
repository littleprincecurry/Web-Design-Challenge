    Average Rating by Year Released


    MyAnimeList refers to this field as 'Air Date Start,' but this includes movies and other types that would not have an "air date start," much less an "air date end." Here, I've pulled the year from each title and averaged my ratings for that year.

    If I were to redo this, I'd either set a range limit on dates because the majority of anime was released in the last 60 years, (although it is interesting to note my earliest title if from the beginning of the twentieth century!) or I would limit it to years were I've ranked a certain number of titles. Regardless, seeing this graph makes me want to seek out more, older anime.
