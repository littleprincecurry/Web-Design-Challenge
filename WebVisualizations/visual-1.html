    Scores


    On MyAnimeList, users can rate a title on a scale from 1 to 10. This graph was made comparing the average user score and my score for titles included in my list. Important to note that my list is only comprised of shows that I have marked as completed, while MyAnimeList allows users to rate a title regardless of completion.

    I expected my scores would not line up exactly with the average user score for many shows and that is mostly true. It's interesting to me that shows I rated a 6 span the entire range of user scores, and that range tightens and trends upwards as my score inceases. I think this graph says more about my tastes not alligning with the general populace more than it says anything about the average user score, meaning, I can probably continute to ignore this feature when looking for anime to watch.
